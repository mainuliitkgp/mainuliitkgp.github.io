About me

I am a Google India Ph.D fellow at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur starting from Jan, 2016. I am working under the supervision of Prof. Niloy Ganguly. My research interest lies in Data Mining, analysis of Online Social Network(OSN). My work majorly involves statistical machine learning, network analysis, and their application to study OSN. I am a part of Complex Network Research Group.
